How we think

What your members are actually asking.

Every enterprise CTO on your board has the same open question right now: which parts of our stack should get an LLM, which should get traditional code, which should get a rule engine, and which should stay human.

Eduba's working frame is sixty / thirty / ten. Sixty percent of most problems are database and workflow work. Thirty percent are rule-based logic. Ten percent are genuine AI problems. Teams that skip this distinction spend a lot of money putting LLMs where Postgres would do the job faster.

That frame is what Jake and our chief architect take into the room. It is what we teach. It is what we build.
